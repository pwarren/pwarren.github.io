---
title: Plum Sauce
tags: recipes Ellen
---


 * 6 lbs egg plums
 * 3 pints vinegar
 * 1 tblsp salt
 * 1 tblsp cloves
 * 1 lb onions
 * 3 lbs sugar
 * 1 tsp cayenne
 * 1 tblsp spice
 * 1 tblsp whole ginger (bruised)

Boil all ingredients together for one hour, then strain through a colander, and bottle when cold.
